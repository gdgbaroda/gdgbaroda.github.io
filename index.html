// 01 — what we do

<about/>

Google Developer Groups Baroda is a technology community that meets to explore the latest Google technologies, tools, SDKs, and APIs — and the open web around them.

We host talks, hands-on workshops, study jams, and our flagship DevFest. Topics span web, cloud, AI/ML, mobile, and more — always welcoming, always technical-but-friendly.

Whether you write code, design, organise, or simply want to learn — there is a place for you on the masthead.
